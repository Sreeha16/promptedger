# Unit Economics

## Lead Value
* **Target:** A startup spending $2,000/mo on AI.
* **Audit Savings:** If we identify a 20% overspend, we save them $400/mo ($4,800/yr).
* **Credex Value:** A converted lead that optimizes their stack via Credex is estimated to be worth $1,000 in annual recurring revenue (ARR) to Credex.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* **Organic Channels:** Twitter/Hacker News = $0.
* **Time Cost:** 2 hours/week of founder engagement = $50 (based on $25/hr opportunity cost).
* **Target CAC:** < $20 per consultation booking.

## Scalability
* **To drive $1M ARR:** At $1,000/lead, we need 1,000 converted consultations. 
* **Conversion Math:** If 5% of audit users book a consultation, we need 20,000 total audits. 
* **Truth:** To reach this, the tool must become a "viral utility" shared on tech forums every time a new AI pricing update drops.
