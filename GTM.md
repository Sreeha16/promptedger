# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target User
My target user is a **Founding Engineer or CTO at a Seed-stage startup (1-10 employees)**. They are currently overwhelmed by managing infrastructure costs while trying to hit product-market fit.

## Channels
1. **The "Hacker News" Strategy:** I will draft a post highlighting the "Hidden AI Tax" startups pay when over-provisioning LLM seats.
2. **Community Outreach:** I will engage in the 'Indie Hackers' and 'YC Founder' Slack groups, offering free audits to founders struggling with their monthly cloud bills.
3. **Twitter/X:** I will share the link to the tool as a "Public Good" resource, specifically tagging AI enthusiasts who are vocal about the high costs of tools like Cursor/Claude.

## Unfair Distribution
Unlike generic "cost calculators," this tool provides a **Credex-backed consultation path** for companies spending >$500/mo. This transforms a free audit into a lead-generation asset. 

## 30-Day Goal
* Week 1: Launch on X and Reddit, targeting 50 audit completions.
* Month 1: Acquire 100+ audit completions and secure 5 consultation bookings.
