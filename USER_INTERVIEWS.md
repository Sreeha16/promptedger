# User Interviews: Insights & Findings

## 1. The "Shadow IT" Problem (Startup Founder, Seed Stage)
* **The Context:** Found that the team was paying for 12 individual ChatGPT Plus subscriptions because no one knew about the "Team" plan options.
* **Key Pain:** "I’m spending $240/mo on redundant subscriptions. It’s not just the money; it’s the lack of centralized admin for our AI tools."
* **The "Aha!" Moment:** The value isn't just the audit; it's the **consolidation**. The tool must explicitly highlight the "Team Plan vs. Individual" savings gap.

## 2. The Productivity-Spend Tradeoff (Senior Software Engineer)
* **The Context:** Engineering team uses Cursor, Copilot, and Claude Pro simultaneously.
* **Key Pain:** "I don't know if I'm getting value out of all these tools. If I'm using Copilot 90% of the time, the others are just 'leakage'."
* **The "Aha!" Moment:** Users don't just want to save money; they want to **prune their toolset**. I need to add a "Recommendation" section that suggests *which* tool to drop based on low usage.

## 3. The Finance Transparency Issue (Operations Manager, Series A)
* **The Context:** Finance team sees "AI Service" as one big line item on the credit card statement, with no idea who is using what.
* **Key Pain:** "We can't optimize what we can't see. I need a clear report that shows me who is spending what so I can hold departments accountable."
* **The "Aha!" Moment:** The tool is more valuable as a **reporting mechanism** for Finance/Ops than just a calculator for a single user.
